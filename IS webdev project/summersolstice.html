Summer Solstice Sunrise

21 June 2023

It's two in the morning, then why can't I sleep?

Thoughts race of what's leaving and what I can keep

Rain patters the window, like tears that will fall

And I ponder the darkness that now on me falls.

Who knew that these feelings could flip like a switch?

Security, peace — so now what's the sitch?

I marvel at the creation of feelings so strong

Have these soul-aching holds been inside all along?

I lay at my fig tree feeling so alone

But I look out and up and remember I'm known

These burdens are heavy, these rocks all my own

But I have a Savior who rolls away stones

My cares are His cares, He's felt what I feel

He knows more than anyone that darkness is real

So He smiles at me through His all-knowing tears

Arms lovingly stretched, He says: "Please, come here"

Yes, His arms full of safety and His hands that have healed

From the blind and the sick to the crops in a field

As He tenderly holds me, he's healing my heart

My sorrows replaced with a joy set apart

"For those who repent, that means come unto me

Your trials I know and your pain I can see

I wait for you here where my job is the best

For those heavy-laden I can provide REST

You know where to turn, child, for I'll never leave

My Spirit's the best gift that you could receive

Its fruits are so many and joyous to the soul

Keeping Him a constant companion should always be your goal

He'll remind you of me, and that I'm always near

And when I need to tell you something he will *whisper in your ear*

He'll tell you when you've been forgiven, you'll be happy when you're clean

This is quite important to me, let me show you what I mean."

His hand slides down my shoulder and He places it in mine

He says, "Our Father made you, you are special, loved, divine.

We love you more than anyone and you should know that's true"

Feeling His hands, I look at Him... He says, "I did it all for you."
